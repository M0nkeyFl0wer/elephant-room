We're Tarah and Ben

Old friends. Longtime collaborators. Seasoned Campaigners.

We've spent the last 20 years organizing campaigns, building tools, running strategy, and supporting communities. Elephant Room is how we keep doing that; with people we trust, on projects that matter.

We prefer to introduce each other

Ben brings focus to chaos. A rare hybrid of policy wonk, systems thinker, and pragmatic problem-solver, he's worked across tech, climate, and Indigenous governance; not just navigating different worlds, but truly connecting them.

Ben builds bridges with humility, never posturing; he just gets the work done and makes everyone around him better at doing theirs. He's allergic to BS, ruthless about impact, and somehow still the most trusted person in the room.

Tarah is a builder. Of movements, bridges, systems, and narratives that actually stick. A storyteller who evokes empathy, a strategist who looks at the entire board, and a pragmatist who'll count buildings if that's what the data gap demands.

She doesn't just talk change: she architects it: But you'll rarely see her coming. Where others see obstacles, she finds hidden levers: a loophole in public opinion, a story that cracks open policy, or the exact pressure point to turn bystanders into allies.
